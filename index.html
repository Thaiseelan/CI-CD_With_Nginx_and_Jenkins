🌍 Global Warming & Its Impact

Act today to save tomorrow

What is Global Warming?

Global warming refers to the long-term rise in Earth’s average temperature, mainly due to human activities like burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial emissions.

Its Major Impacts

🌡️ Rising Temperatures: Heat waves are becoming more frequent and intense.
🌊 Melting Ice & Rising Seas: Glaciers are disappearing, causing sea levels to rise and threatening coastal cities.
🌪️ Extreme Weather: Hurricanes, floods, and droughts are becoming more destructive.
🐧 Loss of Biodiversity: Many species are at risk due to habitat destruction and climate shifts.

What Can We Do?

  • Switch to renewable energy sources 🌞
  • Plant more trees 🌱
  • Use energy-efficient devices 💡
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle ♻️
  • Spread awareness 📢

🌱 Together, we can fight global warming and protect our planet 🌍
